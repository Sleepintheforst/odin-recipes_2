Yogurt

Description

Light and refreshing start to the day. Creamy yogurt topped with juicy berries gives you a balance of protein, natural sweetness, and antioxidants â€” the perfect breakfast to wake you up and fuel your morning.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup Bulgarian Yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of blueberries
  • 1 cup chopped strawberries
  • 1 tbsp honey

Steps

  1. Place 3/4 cup of yogurt in a bowl.
  2. Wash and slice your berries, place on top of the yogurt.
  3. Drizzle honey over top.
  4. Enjoy as an amazing breakfast!
