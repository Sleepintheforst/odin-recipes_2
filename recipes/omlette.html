Reyn's Tasty Omlette with Cucumbers

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3-4 tbsp olive oil
  • 50 g sirene (Lacrima/Vereya/Sayana), crumbled
  • 50 g kashkaval (Sayana/Vereya)
  • A pinch of salt
  • 1 medium cucumber
  • Juice of ½ lemon

Steps for the omlette

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl, whisk them lightly with a fork until smooth.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Sprinkle the crumbled sirene and kashkaval evenly over the eggs.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes, then gently fold the omelette in half.
  5. Continue cooking until the cheese is melted and the omelette is cooked through but still soft inside.
  6. Serve warm.

Steps for the salad

  1. Wash and slice the cucumbers (thin rounds).
  2. Place them in a bowl.
  3. Sprinkle with a pinch of salt.
  4. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over and then drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Toss gently and serve chilled or at room temperature.
